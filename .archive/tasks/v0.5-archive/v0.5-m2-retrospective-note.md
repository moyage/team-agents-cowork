## 3. Canonical Workflow State: Source-of-Truth vs Mirror
**Ruling:** 
- `workflow/dispatch.json` is the **Canonical Source-of-Truth (SoT)** for the Layer 2 Agent Router. It solely dictates the active gate, blocker status, and next expected action. The agent must strictly halt or proceed based on this file.
- `workflow/iteration-state.json` is a **Read-Only Mirror (Log)** operating at Layer 3. It aggregates historical context, completed artifacts, and phase summaries for human/system retrospective, but carries zero routing authority. 
**Resolution:** The two files have been synchronized, with their governance roles strictly defined to prevent future State Drift.
