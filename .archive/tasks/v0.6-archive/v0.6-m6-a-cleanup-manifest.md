# V0.6-M6-B Cleanup Manifest

## 1. Objective
Define the precise mapping of files and directories to be archived, stubbed, or deleted in V0.6-M6-B to complete the Repository Normalization milestone without executing large-scale migrations or breaking active references.

## 2. Archival Actions
- **Target:** `tasks/v0.4-*` and `tasks/v0.5-*` artifacts.
- **Destination:** `.archive/tasks/v0.5-archive/`
- **Exclusions:** Do NOT archive any `tasks/v0.6-*` artifacts.

## 3. Stubbing & Deprecation Actions

  ```markdown
  This document has been superseded and relocated to the unified `documentation/` center. 
  Please refer to `documentation/HUMAN_GUIDES/` or `documentation/AI_GUIDES/` for current information.
  ```
